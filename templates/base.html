Our Services

Appointment Scheduling:

  • Allow users to schedule appointments with service providers (doctors, consultants, etc.) based on their availability.
  • Provide a user-friendly interface for selecting preferred time slots.

User Registration and Authentication:

  • Allow users to create accounts and authenticate themselves.
  • Secure login mechanisms to protect user data.

Provider Management:

  • Enable service providers to manage their availability, set working hours, and define appointment durations.
  • Allow providers to update their profiles with relevant information.

Client Management:

  • Maintain a database of client information, including contact details and appointment history.
  • Enable clients to view and manage their appointments.

Automated Notifications:

  • Send automated reminders to clients and providers about upcoming appointments.
  • Notify users of any changes or cancellations in their scheduled appointments.

Calendar Integration:

  • Integrate with calendars (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.)
